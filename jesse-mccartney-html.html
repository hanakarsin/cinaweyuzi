Jesse McCartney - Age, Family, Bio

Singer and actor who first appeared as JR Chandler on the soap opera All My Children. He later joined the boy band Dream Street before branching into a solo career. McCartney's band's self-titled debut album went Gold in 2001. 

Before Fame

He joined the national King and I tour when he was ten and joined the American pop boy band Dream Street five years later. 

Trivia

He voice-acted as Theodore in the Alvin and the Chipmunks franchise. 

Family Life

He was born to Ginger and Scott McCartney, who encouraged him to perform in local community musicals when he was seven. He is an older brother to siblings Lea and Tim. He proposed to Katie Peterson in September 2019; the two wed in October 2021.

Associated With

He and Anne Hathaway sang a duet for Ella Enchanted titled "Don't Go Breaking My Heart," which was also featured on the film's soundtrack.
