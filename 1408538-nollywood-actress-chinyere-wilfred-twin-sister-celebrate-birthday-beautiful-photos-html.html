Nollywood Actress Chinyere Wilfred and Twin Sister Celebrate Birthday with Beautiful Photos

- Chinyere Wilfred is celebrating 51 years on earth and took to her Instagram page to mark the big day

- In one of the photos, the twins stunned in a hooded dress that was cut in the same style
