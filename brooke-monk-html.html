Brooke Monk - Age, Family, Bio

Lip sync and dance content creator known for posting short videos on her brookemonk_ TikTok account. Most of her videos feature relatable content for the average high school student. She has over 18 million fans on the platform. 

Before Fame

She designed an iPhone case in an early TikTok.

Trivia

She hosted a fan Q&A on her self-titled YouTube channel in November 2019. She earned over 4 million views for an April 2020 TikTok video where she imitated her parents trying to interpret a meme.  She signed with talent agency WME in March of 2022.

Family Life

She has four sisters and she's the middle child. She started dating fellow social media star Sam Dezz in October 2020.

Associated With

She joined the Hype House in March 2022 and left in June 2022.
