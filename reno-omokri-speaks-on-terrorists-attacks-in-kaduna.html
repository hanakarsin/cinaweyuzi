Reno Omokri Speaks On Terrorists Attacks In Kaduna

Former presidential aide, Reno Omokri has blamed President Muhammadu Buhari for the recent attacks in Kaduna State.

The activist claimed that terrorists are attacking communities in Kaduna because President Buhari lived in the state from 1989 till 2015 when he became the President of Nigeria.

Omokri also condemned the co-coordinator of Bring Back Our Girls campaign, Oby Ezekwesili for protesting against Goodluck Jonathan which led to his removal from office in 2015.

Ezekwesili had marched against Jonathan following the abduction of students from Chibok boarding school In 2014.

President Buhari in 2015 promised to bring back the girls once he became President but some of the girls are still in captivity up till now.

In recent times more than 2000 students have been kidnapped from different places in the North West

Omokri wondered why Oby Ezekwesili has not started a campaign against the Buhari government.

He said, “Nigerians think. Why do you think these terrorists are concentrated in Kaduna? That is where Buhari lived from 1989 until he became President in 2015.

“He was grand patron of Miyetti Allah. Do Google it. And many of these terrorists are offshoots of Miyetti Allah!”

On Oby, he wrote, “Almost 2000 children have been abducted from schools by terrorists under Buhari. More than under Jonathan.

“If @ObyEzeks interest was altruistic, how come she has not advocated for them like #ChibokGirls? How come #BringBackOurGirls died out after Buhari won in 2015?”
