Lulu Wilson - Age, Family, Bio

Child actress who became known for her role as Mikayla on the show The Millers. She has also starred in the horror films, Ouija: Origin of Evil and The Haunting of Hill House. Other film credits include Deliver Us from Evil and Her Composition.

Before Fame

She started acting when she was three years old, appearing in commercials and working as a voice actress.

Trivia

She's appeared in episodes of Black Box, Inside Amy Schumer, and Teachers. 

Family Life

She grew up in New York City. She's featured her two cats on her Instagram account.

Associated With

She appeared alongside J.B. Smoove on The Millers.
