Man uses G-Wagon to carry watermelons

In another story that left Kenyans stunned, a man was spotted carrying loads of watermelons at the back of his Mercedes Benz G-Wagon., something you do not see every day.

In the video that went viral, the man recording the clip was stunned to see a friend, who he referred to as Maasai, offloading watermelons loaded in the boot.

"This is a 30 million G-Wagon, but there's a surprise," he said as he went from the front of the car to the back, displaying two men offloading the fruits.

He hilariously asked Maasai why he was using the sporty ride for such a task, and the owner dismissed him like it was nothing.

Some Kenyans were amazed by the scenes, hilariously asking where he got the audacity to do such a thing.
