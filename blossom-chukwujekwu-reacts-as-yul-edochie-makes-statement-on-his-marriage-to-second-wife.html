Blossom Chukwujekwu Reacts As Yul Edochie Makes Statement On His Marriage To Second Wife

Nollywood actor Blossom Chukwujekwu has hailed his colleague Yul Edochie for extending some prayers to his marriage with Pastor Chris Oyakhilome’s niece, Winifred Akhuemokhan, two years after his marriage with estranged wife, Maureen Esisi crashed over alleged infidelity.

It would be recalled that the couple who got engaged on Valentine’s day, February 14, 2022 had their traditional marriage in Benin, Edo state over the weekend stirring reactions on social media space.

Reacting to the wedding of Blossom, the father of five took to his social media page to share photo of Blossom and his new wife, Winifred.

He prayed for the union, asking God to bless the couple and their marriage together.

An excited Blossom took to the comment section of the post to hail Yul, thanking him for the prayers and kind wishes.
