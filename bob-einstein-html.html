Bob Einstein - Trivia, Family, Bio

Emmy award-winning actor best known for his appearances as fictional stuntman, Super Dave Osborne on shows like Late Night with David Letterman. He also had a recurring role on HBO's Curb Your Enthusiasm, appearing in almost two dozen episodes.

Before Fame

He began his career as a writer for The Smothers Brothers Comedy Hour, for which he won an Emmy Award.

Trivia

He earned an Emmy for Outstanding Comedy Series in 1977 as a producer for the series Van Dyke and Company.

Family Life

His younger brother is famed comedic writer and actor, Albert Brooks. His father was a comic and his mother was a singer and actress.

Associated With

He has a recurring role as Marty Funkhouser on Curb Your Enthusiasm with Larry David.
