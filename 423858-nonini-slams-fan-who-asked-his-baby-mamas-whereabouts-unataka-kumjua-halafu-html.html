Nonini Slams Fan Who Asked for His Baby Mama's Whereabouts: &quot;Unataka Kumjua Halafu?&quot;

  • A curious follower wanted to know where Nonini's baby mama has been
  • The rapper wondered why the fan wanted to know his baby mama and told him off in the best way possible
  • Nonini had shared a photo of himself spending time with his son in a swimming pool

Legendary Kenyan rapper Hubert Nakitare alias Nonini is one of the most respected celebrities in the country.

The rapper is known for his scandal-free lifestyle and how he has been dealing with issues in diplomatic ways.

However, the rapper was recently forced to bring out the ''beast'' in him after one of his social media followers shot a controversial question at him.

Nonini had shared a photo of himself enjoying some quality time with his only son in a swimming pool, followed by a short and clear caption.

"The dad and his son," Nonini wrote.

Well, fellow celebrities and his fans camped on the comments section of his post to laud him for being the great dad he is.

Curious follower

One of his followers, however, was curious and wanted to know where Nonini's baby mama has been all this while, holding the rapper has only been showing off the son but never the mum.

"Where is this kid's mother. Because it's always just the two of you." the fan asked.

The question did not settle down well with Nonini who came for the follower's neck.

In a quick rejoinder, the rapper wondered, rather asked the curious fan why he wanted to know about his baby mama.

"What is your problem? You want to know her then? You are a man, you are not supposed to be asking such questions," Nonini responded.

His response was supported by a multitude of his fans who told off the curious follower.

Nonini had earlier disclosed he had been vaccinated for COVID-19 in two different countries.

Double vaccine

The rapper said he was vaccinated in Kenya and still got another jab in the US where he had gone to visit his son.

The musician shared a picture of him on Instagram after receiving his second jab in Baltimore, Maryland, US, disclosing that he got two different vaccine brands for his first and second jab.

He noted that his first dose was the AstraZeneca vaccine in Kenya before leaving for the US.

Many of his followers were inspired and shared their experiences working on getting vaccinated and those of others.
