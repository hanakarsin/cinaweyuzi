Huduma centers in Nairobi locations

In 2013, the government of Kenya launched the Huduma Kenya to enable efficiency in Pubic service delivery. Other objectives of the Huduma Kenya programme is to save time, curtail corruption, and minimize the costs of service delivery. Huduma Kenya services are available through five integrated platforms: Huduma Center, Online e-huduma portal, m-huduma (for mobile phones), Huduma call centers and multi-channel huduma payment gateway. Huduma Centers act as a one-stop shop where Kenyans can access multiple government services. There are about 45 Huduma Centers located across the country. Last year, the government scaled up the reach of the Huduma services by introducing the Huduma Mashinani services targeting each sub-county.

Huduma Center services offered

You can get up to 45 different services at the Huduma Center Services. Some of the Huduma Center services include

  • Huduma center ID replacement
  • Huduma center certificate of good conduct
  • HELB – Student Loan Application and Repayments
  • Issuance of Single Business Permit
  • Registration of Welfare Groups
  • NHIF – Member Registration Claims and Benefits
  • NSSF -Member Registration, Statement, and Claims
  • Status of Pension Claims
  • Stamp Duty Assessment and Payment
  • Motor Vehicle Log Book Search and Driver License Status
  • NCC Seasonal Parking Tickets
  • Search and Registration of Business Names

Huduma Center online services are available through e-Citizen. For example, when applying for a certificate of good conduct, you will have first to create an account with e-Citizen, make payments, fill in your details and print the receipt. You then visit the Huduma Centers to finalize the application processes.

Huduma Center Nairobi

Huduma Centers are strategically located in the capital to help citizens access services easily. Below is a list of all Huduma Centers in Nairobi

1. Huduma genter GPO Nairobi

The GPO Huduma Office is at the Teleposta Building, Kenyatta Avenue in the Nairobi Central Business District.

2. Huduma center city square

City Square office is along Haile Selassie Avenue, Next to the Technical University of Kenya and opposite Times Towers. For Huduma Center Westland's services, you can visit the GPO or City Square offices.

3. Huduma center makadara

The Eastlands Revenue Building houses the Huduma Center Makadara Offices. The building is next to the DC Makadara and Makadara Law Courts off Jogoo Road.
