Robert Jay Perez - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator on the YouTube channel Kubz Scouts who found most of his audience with his Yandere Simulator videos. He has more than 4.5 million subscribers. His "HOW TO GET ZOMBIE TEACHERS! | Yandere Simulator Myths" video has received over 8.8 million views since May of 2016. 

Before Fame

He created the channel in June of 2014 with some of his earliest videos coming from the video game Grand Theft Auto.

Trivia

He previously used to post to a secondary YouTube channel called Backubz Account. He has also posted videos about the game Among Us on his YouTube channel.

Family Life

Born in Virginia, he lived in Japan for over ten years before moving to California. His former girlfriend and fellow gamer, Nini Hebron, came up with his YouTube channel name, Kubz Scouts. He and Nini dated for about ten years before breaking up in early 2020. They have a son together named Mason. 

Associated With

In August 2017, he posted a video to YouTube titled "Listening To Kpop For The First Time (It Changed my life)," in which he listened to music by Jungkook's popular band Bangtan Boys.
