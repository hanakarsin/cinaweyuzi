Kellita Smith - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who played Bernie's wife Wanda on the popular comedy series The Bernie Mac Show. She was also the first Lady Katherine Johnson on the sitcom The First Family in 2012. In 2014, she began playing the character Warren on the Syfy series Z Nation. 

Before Fame

She first acted in an episode of Living Color.

Trivia

She was nominated for NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Actress in the comedy series: The Bernie Mac Show and The First Family. 

Family Life

She grew up with family in Chicago, Illinois, where she performed in local productions. She was born in Chicago, Illinois but raised in Oakland, California.

Associated With

She played a role in The Crossing Guard with Jack Nicholson.
