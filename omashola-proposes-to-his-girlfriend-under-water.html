Omashola Proposes To His Girlfriend Under Water

Former BB Naija housemate, Omashola has proposed to his heartthrob while in the water.

The reality TV star shared a video via his Instagram account. He said the highlight of his year was when his girlfriend agreed to be his wife.

He wrote: “2021 has a lot of secrets but my biggest highlight was when she said YES and the rest is history. Please don’t ask me what happened.

Happy New Year.”

In other news, Zazu Zeh crooner, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable, has received a Range Rover as gift days after acquiring his first car.

The fast-rising Nigerian singer disclosed this in a video he shared on his Instagram page on New Year day, Naija News reports.

Portable’s latest hit ‘Zazoo Zeh’ which featured award-winning singer, Olamide, and Poco Lee is currently the anthem in Nigerian streets. Showing an act of gratitude to his benefactor for the Range Rover gift, Portable wrote, “Tears of joy. Thank you Sir I really appreciate it.”
