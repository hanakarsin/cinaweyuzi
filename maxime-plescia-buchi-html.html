Maxime Plescia-Buchi - Age, Family, Bio

Tattoo artist who founded the influential tattoo studio, publishing house and contemporary art magazine Sang Bleu. He is known on Instagram for his account tttism, where he shares striking and original tattoo designs with more than 20,000 followers. 

Before Fame

His career in art was sparked by an early interest in street graffiti. He studied psychology and graphic design in college. 

Trivia

He was an apprentice under Filip Leu at the legendary studio Leu Family's Family Iron.

Family Life

He married an American woman. 

Associated With

Like Manuela Soto, he is a famous Swiss tattoo artist.
