Have you ever heard of the popular Kenyan phrase ''kamati ya roho chafu'' and ''kamati ya mutawachana tuu''?

If you have not, these are groups that play home to jealousy. They are people who do not want others to prosper, be it careerwise or even in their courtship.

Kenyans now believe voluptuous social Corazon Kwamboka is a member of the latter group after incriminating evidence against her popped up.

Netizens, being netizens, recently went through Corazon Kwamboka's new man Frankie Just Gym It's Instagram account and unveiled several old photos of the well-build lad with his ex-lover Maureen Waititu.

What caught many by surprise is that Corazon was actually following Frankie on social media when he was still with the mother of his two kids.

The funniest bit is that she was also their biggest fan, and this she proved by liking, rather double-tapping several photos of Frankie and Maureen all looking loved up during their days.

Others, however, held she was innocent and liking Frankie's photos may just have been out of genuine fan love.

The latest turn of events comes about seven months after Corazon confirmed she was dating Frankie, and they were actually expecting their first baby together.

The socialite made the reveal during an Instagram live with YouTuber Edgar Obare.

According to Frankie and Kwamboka, the two met when they were down and had just left their previous relationship but only started dating seriously in December 2020.
