Melissa Joan Hart - Age, Family, Bio

Actress known for starring on the television shows Clarissa Explains It All and Sabrina the Teenage Witch, and later Melissa & Joey with Joey Lawrence. In 2018, she was cast to star as Liz in No Good Nick. In 2019, her addition to Nickelodeon's The Casagrandes marked her return to the network since Clarissa Explains It All.

Before Fame

Her acting career began after she participated in a commercial for a bathtub doll at age four. 

Trivia

She competed with Mark Ballas on Dancing with the Stars in 2009. She won a game of Celebrity Family Feud in 2016.  

Family Life

She married Mark Wilkerson in 2003 and together they had three children. Actress Emily Hart is her younger sister.

Associated With

She starred with Caroline Rhea and Beth Broderick on Sabrina the Teenage Witch.
