Gabriela Moura - Age, Family, Bio

Dance and lip-sync content creator who rose to fame by sharing performances on her gabimfmoura account. She has more than 8 million followers on the platform. She has been especially skilled at the belly dancing style of choreography. 

Before Fame

She made her TikTok debut in March of 2020. One of her first dances is set to music by Blueface.

Trivia

She has also cultivated a following on Instagram, where she posts selfies and modeling photos. She has also been vocal about body image issues on her Instagram page and talked about the unrealistic expectations of social media. 

Family Life

She was born in Rio de Janeiro. She danced with her dog in a September 2020 TikTok video.

Associated With

She performed a TikTok dance to Popp Hunna's "Adderall (Corvette Corvette)."
