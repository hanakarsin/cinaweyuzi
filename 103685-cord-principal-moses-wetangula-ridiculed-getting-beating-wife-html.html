Cord principal Moses Wetangula ridiculed after getting a beating from wife

- Both the senator and his wife reported to Langata Police Station claiming that they had been injured; the case has since been transferred to Karen Police Station

Kenyans on Twitter on Monday March 7, 2016 made Cord's principal Moses Wetangula a laughing stock following news of a domestic brawl with his wife.

Over the weekend, the Sunday Nation edition on March 6 had reported that Wetangula and his wife Ann had accused each other of assault and both the senator and wife recorded statements at Lang'ata police station.

According to the report, Wetangula is said to have accused his wife of infidelity.

He said it is at that point when she got furious and attacked him.

He is said to have sustained injuries on his head, left hand, left leg and chest.

He was treated at Karen Hospital and issued with a P3 form.

Police surgeon confirmed that Wetangula sustained multiple soft tissue injuries.

The surgeon said he had a swelling on the left side of his scalp and injuries on the chest, wrist, elbow and ankle which could have been caused by a blunt object.

The Lang'ata police officers are said to have transferred the issue to Karen Police Station as that is where the alleged incident took place.
