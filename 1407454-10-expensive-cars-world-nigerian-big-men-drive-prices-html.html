Adorable Photos of 10 Most Expensive Cars in the World Nigerian Big Men Drive and their Prices

When it comes to cars with big budgets, the conversation shifts from them just being a means of transportation to things like design, speed, and comfort.

Legit.ng compiled a list of 10 most expensive cars according to Boxymo. Below are their prices and the things that make them special:

1. Koenigsegg CCXR Trevita - $4.8m (N1,827,600,000)

The car has a 4.8 liter combined with a double maxi-charged V8 which makes it perfect when it overtakes on the freeway without the fear of an accident

2. Lamborghini Veneno ($4.5M) (N1,713,375,000)

This vehicle looks beautiful and sleek, whichever angle you look at it from. Many say that it looks more like a spacecraft than an actual car.

3. W Motors Lykan Hypersport - $3.4M (1,294,550,000)

Coming third on the list, Hypersport became really popular when it starred in Fast and Furious 7. In the movie, the car crashed through skyscrapers

4. Mansory Vivere Bugatti Veyron - $3.4M (N1,294,550,000)

The Bugatti Veyron is coveted for its great speed. The whopping prize makes well for the power it commands on the road.

5. Ferrari F60 America - $2.5M (N951,875,000)

This vehicle has a striking resemblance to the F12. However, its 6.2-litre V12 and 740 great horsepower differentiate it.

6. Koenigsegg One:1 - $2.0M (N761,500,000)

Though there are a lot of things one could do with that amount of money, Koenigsegg 1 could be a great buy with its engine configuration that places it well on a good pedestal.

7. Aston Martin One-77 - $1.4M (N533,050,000)

Looking well-built, this car commands an aspirated V12 engine. It also produces 750 horsepower. Those numbers make the One-77 the fastest Aston Martin ever made.

8. Pagani Huayra - $1.4M (N533,050,000)

It has an AMG-sourced 6.0-liter V12 with two huge turbochargers. It is really worth its price tag.

Its weight is below 2,800 pounds. The car can go from 0 to 60 mph within three seconds. It can go as high as 217 mph.

It has a 6.8 litre engine combined with 1,104 horsepower, it greatly commands so much speed power.
