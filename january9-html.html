January 9 Horoscope and Zodiac

The Capricorn's ruling planet is Saturn, but as you were born in the second Decan, or part, of the sign, you also receive a dose of Venus's mysterious planetary power. Being the planet of control, Saturn's influence is responsible for your disincline and determination. In the same light, it is Venus that is linked to your desire for harmony, cooperation and sociability. Your unique combination of planetary influences makes you more social and creatively inclined than the other Capricorn Decans. In all aspects of life, you have the natural abilities to solve any problem you encounter. You determination for success is met with an equal devotion to relationships and love. Once you find a worthwhile mate, you will put sizable effort into cultivating a healthy relationships. For the most happiness, find a partner that shares in your commitment to loyalty, attention and affection.
