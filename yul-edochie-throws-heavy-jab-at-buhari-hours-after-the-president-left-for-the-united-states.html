Yul Edochie Throws Heavy Jab At Buhari Hours After The President Left For The United States

Nollywood actor, Yul Edochie has berated Nigerian President, Muhammad Buhari over his incessant travels while his people suffer in Nigeria. 

Naija News reports that President Muhammadu Buhari on Sunday departed Nigeria for the United States to attended the United Nations, General Assembly.

In the reports, Buhari is scheduled to speak to world leaders during the 76th Session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA76) in New York.

Buhari waves goodbye to Nigeria as he heads for UNGA

Apart from the UN meeting, the President will also hold some high-level meetings and interactions while in the United States.

Taking to his Instagram, Yul Edochie expressed his displeasure with the state of how things currently are in the country, lamenting bitterly how the country’s leaders, both present and past have wrecked the nation by stealing the billions of naira meant for the commonwealth of the people. 

Yul Edochie.

He said it doesn’t make sense that the president is always out of the country every other weekend while the people he governs wallows in poverty and sickness. 

He urged the President to fix his county and stop going to countries where the leaders have made the efforts of putting their country together.

He added that Nigerians should start thinking well by dropping unhealthy emotions, such as hate, jealously, envy, tribalism, and habits as no one will be leaving this world alive.
