Qui Reid - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star who is famous as one-half of the Qui & Ken YouTube channel. She has gained popularity there alongside her long-time boyfriend Ken for their pranks, challenges and couples' vlogging. She is active on her quirenea Instagram account, where she posts fashion and lifestyle pictures and videos.

Before Fame

She and Ken launched their YouTube channel in April 2013 with the intent to help motivate people.

Trivia

She has gained more than 1.5 million subscribers to her YouTube channel. She is sponsored by FLAT TUMMY CO, and features their products on her Instagram.

Family Life

She and her boyfriend Ken, AKA therealitsken on Instagram, began vlogging together on YouTube in December 2017. 

Associated With

She surprised her boyfriend Ken in a prank video by hiding in the trunk of a car dressed as Penny Wise from the movie It.
