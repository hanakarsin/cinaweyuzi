Park Seo-joon - Age, Family, Bio

Known for his starring roles in such South Korean television series as Witch's Romance, Pots of Gold, and Dream High 2. He also served as the host of KBS variety program Music Bank. His role as Park Hyun-tae in Pots of Gold earned him a 2013 Korea Drama Award.

Before Fame

He made his screen debut in the music video for singer Bang Yong-guk's popular song "I Remember."

Trivia

In 2012 and 2013, he played the role of Cha Seo-joon in the KBS2 series Shut Up Family.

Family Life

He is the oldest of three brothers. In 2023, it was reported that he was dating YouTuber xooos. 

Associated With

He appeared with Ji Jin-hee in the SBS series One Warm Word.
