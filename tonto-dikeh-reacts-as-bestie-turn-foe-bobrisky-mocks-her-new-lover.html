Tonto Dikeh Reacts As Bestie Turn Foe, Bobrisky Mocks Her New Lover

Nollywood actress, Tonto Dikeh has reacted to the shade her former best friend, Bobrisky threw at her, whilst warning her fans to be careful of jealous people who often appear to be supportive angels. 

The mother of one advised her fans to be watchful and guarded of jealous people who appear to be supportive angels after Nigerian crossdresser, Bobrisky took to his Instagram page to shade Tonto Dikeh following her new relationship with politician boyfriend, Prince Kpokpgri.

Naija News recalls that Bobrisky, via Instagram, tagged Tonto as always depressed and consistently showing off fake lifestyles on social media.

He wrote; There you go again, Mrs always depressed. Fake life dey her body. Wonder when she will learn. They only post what people want to see. They will never post what they suffer indoor. I’m done with Instagram Damn.

Reacting to the post, Tonto responded; ”A jealous demon may appear as a supportive angel. Be watchful!! Be GUARDED MY PEOPLE”
