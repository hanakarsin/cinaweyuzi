Boni Khalwale Shows Off Impressive Luhya Dance Moves During Radio Interview

  • Boni Khalwale was invited to an interview at Radio Ingo, and his words were not the only thing that left fans impressed
  • The politician showed great energy as he took the floor for a dance alongside the radio host
  • The energy and ease of movement left many impressed as they lauded Khalwale, with others offering him advice

Kakamega gubernatorial aspirant Boni Khalwale is known for his tough political stance and love for dance. He has a soft spot for tunes from his Luhya culture.

The politician has shown that he is not losing his dance moves anytime soon, especially with the general election around the corner.

In a video shared on his Twitter account, Khalwale paid a courtesy call at Radio Ingo on Wednesday, July 21, for an interview.

In the video, the former Kakamega senator could be seen showing off some moves alongside the radio host before they sat down to chat.

As they danced to some Luhya tunes, the bullfighter (as he is famously known by his admirers) showed off his 'mabeka' (shoulders) moves that were full of energy.

He led as the radio host followed with the two jumping up and down and went side to side with a smile on his face as he did what he probably considers a hobby.

Khalwale praises Azziad Nasenya

About a month ago, Khalwale lauded a video of TikTok sensation turned radio presenter Azziad dancing to a Luhya tune.

Boni came across a lovely video of Azziad, which was shared on Twitter by @luhya_pounds, of her dancing to Emmanuel Musindi's Sena Lwanyi and the account also tagged him.

The 21-year-old was rocking a bridal gown that perfectly fitted her and pulled some 'mabeka' moves like a professional Luhya dancer.

With a huge smile on her face and lip-syncing parts of the song, the beauty showed why she is the ultimate TikTok dance queen.

Khalwale reshared the video while praising Azziad in one word: "Fantastic."
