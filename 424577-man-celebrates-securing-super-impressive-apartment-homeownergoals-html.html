  • A local man has social media in a frenzy after sharing a snap of the keys to his new home

A man in South Africa has social media buzzing after sharing a glimpse into his brand new apartment and the keys to the front door.

Heading online, @yoboiistunna shared a snap of his sleek new crib.

Latest landlord in town

Recently, a Nigerian man marked becoming the latest landlord in town as he announced the completion of his house.

The architect identified as Darlington Chidiebere Opara had shared photos at intervals on his Facebook page highlighting the start and finish of the beautiful bungalow but hadn't revealed the house under construction was his.

Now breaking the silence on the actual owner of the property he had been working on, Darlington said he acquired the land and built on it within the space of 12 months.
