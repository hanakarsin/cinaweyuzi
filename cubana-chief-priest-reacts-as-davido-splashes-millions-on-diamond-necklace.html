Cubana Chief Priest Reacts As Davido Splashes Millions On Diamond Necklace

Popular media personality and businessman, Pascal Chibuike Okechukwu, also known as Cubana Chief Priest has praised Nigerian singer, Davido for purchasing a diamond necklace reportedly worth about £500,000.

Naija News reports that Cubana on Wednesday via his verified Instagram handle shared a picture of Davido flaunting his new necklace.

He captioned the photo, “Nwanne The Ice💎 Is Pouring⏳Leave Lifestyle💸 For 30BG.”

Recall that the ‘Feel’ crooner via a post on Twitter on Tuesday, revealed the price of his new necklace and also stated that he is going into the second phase of his tour.

“Timeless piece being delivered tomoro right in time for 2nd phase of tour !!  … spent bout half a mill £ on it … God is Good,” he wrote.

The DMW record label owner acquired the diamond necklace to celebrate the success of his album Timeless which was released in March.

Davido ‘Mocks’ El-Rufai’s New Photo

Meanwhile, Davido has reacted to the viral new photo of the former Kaduna governor, Nasir El-Rufai.

The former FCT minister had via his social media handle confirmed his current location following controversy about his whereabouts after the alleged withdrawal of his ministerial appointment.

He shared a photo on Tuesday, revealing his location with the caption: “Chilling in Beirut”

In reaction to the photo, Davido, via the comment section of a news blog shared a laughing emoji, implying how ‘hurt’ the politician was looking in the photo.

The singer rather than using the word ‘hot’ wrote ‘He hurt’ instead.
