Gusttavo Lima - Age, Family, Bio

Latin pop singer who broke onto the music scene with his 2011 album Gusttavo e voce. He achieved international recognition for his single, "Balada."

Before Fame

He signed his first record contract in 2009 after performing as a choir soloist as a teenager.

Trivia

His voice is often compared to that of world music singer Luan Santana. His YouTube channel is immensely popular, with his videos on the platform earning an incredible 16.2 billion total views. By May 2022, his Spotify audience had grown to reach over 9 million monthly listeners. 

Family Life

He was born in Presidente Olegário, MG, Brazil. He had a romantic relationship with Karen Kounrouzan from 2011 to 2012. He married Andressa Suita in 2015. 

Associated With

He gained a large following in Brazil after soccer star Neymar supported his music.
