Patrick Wilson (Movie Actor) - Age, Family, Bio

Actor best known for his work in drama. His credits include Watchmen, Little Children, Insidious, and The Conjuring. He also portrayed Joe Pitt in the HBO miniseries Angels in America.

Before Fame

He made his big screen debut in the 2001 film My Sister's Wedding, playing the character Quinn.

Trivia

He played Joshua in the 2013 episode of the HBO series Girls entitled "One Man's Trash."

Family Life

He married Polish-American actress Dagmara Dominczyk on June 18, 2005. They had their first son, Kalin, in 2006 and their second son, Kassian, in 2009.

Associated With

He starred alongside Vera Farmiga in the 2013 horror film The Conjuring.
