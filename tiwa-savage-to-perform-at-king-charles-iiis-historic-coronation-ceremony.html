Tiwa Savage To Perform At King Charles IIIs Historic Coronation Ceremony

Nigerian female sensational singer, Tiwa Savage, has been billed to perform at the historical coronation ceremony of King Charles III on May 7, 2023.

Naija News reports that the king will officially be crowned on Saturday, May 6 and a Coronation Concert and Coronation Big Lunch will also take place on Sunday.

Buckingham Palace in a statement released on Sunday, April 30, disclosed that Tiwa Savage will join other notable celebrities across the globe, such as Paloma Faith, Steve Winwood, Olly Murs, and DJ Pete Tong, who will play his famous Ibiza classics.

The concert will showcase world-renowned pianists Lang Lang and Lucy, the recent winner of ‘The Piano’ competition.

The festive celebration which would last three days is being held in honor of King Charles, who ascended to the throne after the passing of his mother, Queen Elizabeth II, last year.

The festivities will also include several notable events, such as a concert featuring famous celebrities at Windsor Castle, a series of street parties that will take place throughout the country, and a volunteering campaign called “The Big Help Out.”

The statement reads, “Buckingham Palace is pleased to announce further details on the ceremonial, celebratory, and community events that will take place over the Coronation Weekend between Saturday 6th and Monday 8th May 2023”.

“The Service will be conducted by the Archbishop of Canterbury. As previously announced, the Service will reflect the Monarch’s role today and look towards the future while being rooted in longstanding traditions and pageantry.”
