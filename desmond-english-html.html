MightyDuck - Age, Family, Bio

Video content creator on YouTube who initially made a name for himself through his 6-second videos on Vine. His real name is Desmond English, but he is best known by the internet persona MightyDuck. He earned over 2 million followers on Vine and his YouTube channel has amassed over 2.2 million subscribers.

Before Fame

He scoured social networks, plotting unique ways to try and make people laugh before finding his comedic voice on Vine.

Trivia

He is best known for his pranking videos.

Family Life

He was born in Atlanta, Georgia. He has two sisters who are also popular on social media, MightyNeicy and Ermani. He and his wife Elizabeth have a son named Channing and a daughter named Emmalyn.

Associated With

He's often collaborated with Head Capone and Chaz Williams.
