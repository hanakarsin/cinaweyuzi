Michael Thurston - Age, Family, Bio

English fitness guru who co-founded the health and fitness company Aurora Athletic. He is quite popular on YouTube, where his vlogs and training videos have earned him more than 1.1 million subscribers to date.

Before Fame

He grew up in Leeds, Yorkshire, England playing a variety of variety of sports, including soccer, rugby, and basketball. He studied economics and business management at Newcastle University.

Trivia

He competed in the WBFF shows in London in 2014 and 2015, placing third in the muscle model category.

Family Life

He has a brother. 

Associated With

He has taken Instagram photos with Ex on the Beach star Rogan O'Connor.
