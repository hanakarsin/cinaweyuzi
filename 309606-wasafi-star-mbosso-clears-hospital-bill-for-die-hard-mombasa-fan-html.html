Wasafi star Mbosso clears hospital bill for die-hard Mombasa fan

Kenya and Tanzania recently got into a tussle following remarks by Kenyan politician Jaguar, who in a recent public rally requested all Tanzanians and Chinese be deported.

His statement caused a rift between citizens from the two countries with each party throwing shade at the other.

Well, in the midst of all the melee, WCB singer Mbosso decided to do some little bit of damage control by going all philanthropic for one of his ailing Kenyan fans.

According to Ommydallah, Mbosso cleared hospital bill for his die hard fan known as Jamal Ismail who had been admitted at the Coast General Hospital.

Jamal, according to report, had been at the hospital for two month after he was admitted following health complications which saw his leg, the only one remaining amputated.

Mbosso who is a constant guest in the country is said to have coughed about KSh 150,000 to clear Jamal's accumulated hospital bill.

Mbosso's act of good deed was confirmed by Mombasa-based radio presenter and actress Mama Madikodiko who thanked the singer immensely for his big heart.

"Mbosso has been very helpful, he has always been in constant communication with Jamal through me throughout the period that he had beenn admitted. We pray that God rewards him for this " said Madikodiko.

Ommydallah went ahead to reveal Mama Madikodiko was the one who was playing the link between the star and Jamal.
