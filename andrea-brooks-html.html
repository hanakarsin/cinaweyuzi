Andrea Brooks (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber and lifestyle coach best known for the fashion tips and do-it-yourself videos she uploads to her channel AndreasChoice. The channel has earned over 4 million subscribers. She has posted numerous videos detailing her daily routines. 

Before Fame

She had an interest in art as a child and often illustrated her own comic books.

Trivia

She became the first beauty-themed channel to be signed to Maker Studios in 2010, the same year she moved to L.A. She has also posted her art on her art-dedicated andydreamy Instagram page. 

Family Life

She has a twin sister named Brittany and a brother named Anthony.

Associated With

Her work was featured on Perez Hilton's CocoPerez Blog in March 2011.
