Big Brother Naija To Commence Audition For Season 5, See Date

Big Brother Naija, BBNaija reality show has announced the date for auditioning housemates for its next season.

Through its verified Twitter page, the organizers announced that the show’s auditioning would begin on May 20th and end on the 30th.

However, suspectedly due to the Coronavirus pandemic, the organisers have asked that participants interested in the reality show to upload a video introducing themselves and giving reasons for their interest in the show.

The tweet said the audition will be open to young adults above 21years, with valid Nigerian passports.
