Reactions As Supreme Court Rules Against Buhari

Nigerians have reacted to the ruling of the Supreme Court on Friday declaring Executive Order 10 issued by President Muhammadu Buhari as unlawful and unconstitutional.

Naija News reports that six out of the seven-member panel of the Supreme Court ruled against the executive order and gave victory to the state governors who had challenged the declaration by the President.

It held that it was not the responsibility of the Federal Government to fund the capital and recurrent expenditures of the superior courts created for States under Section six of the Constitution.

This online platform reports that the order is for the implementation of Financial Autonomy for the State Legislature and Judiciary.

The apex court by its ruling has now set aside executive order 10 also known as the financial autonomy order.

President Buhari according to the order, intends that the legislative and judicial arms of government in the 36 states of the federation will no longer have to wait on State governors for funds.

The President argued that his decision was based on Section 5 of the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria 1999 (as Amended).

The order has now been declared void by a Supreme Court decision, eliciting different reactions from Nigerians on social media.
