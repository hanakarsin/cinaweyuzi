Elderly Man Appreciates Femi Otedola after Billionaire Sponsored Medical Treatment

  • A Nigerian man identified as Dr Ini Ebong has taken to social media to thank billionaire, Femi Otedola, for his assistance
  • The man, in August 2020, had fallen sick following a heart condition and after crying out to the public, via an NGO and the media, Otedola came to his rescue
  • Several Nigerians have taken to the comment section to shower praises and blessings on the billionaire for coming to his rescue

F is back on his feet and it is all thanks to popular Nigerian billionaire, Femi Otedola, who through his charity foundation, came to the aide of Ebong.

Forceful retirement and subsequent illness

According to Vanguard, Ebong who until his retirement in 2002, was an Associate Professor, Department of Theatre Arts, University of Uyo in Akwa Ibom State.

In August of 2020, he fell sick and, according to the medical report, Ebong was brought to the hospital with congestive cardiac failure.

Femi Otedola reached out to Ebong

The news was published by Premium Times. Shortly after, Femi Otedola reached out to assist the lecturer through the foundation.

In a recent video shared on Instagram by Dele Momodu, a frail Ebong walking without assistance as he thanked the billionaire for coming to his rescue.

Vanguard also revealed that Ebong was ‘forcefully’ and ‘unjustly’ retired by the university authorities in 2002.

Despite a string of victories in court, following his several legal battles with the institution, he has been consistently denied reinstatement.
