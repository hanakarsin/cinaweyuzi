Sunday LaBrant - Age, Family, Bio

Gained fame from birth as the daughter of social stars Savannah and Cole LaBrant. Her parents posted a video capturing the day leading up to her birth and the moments following her birth to their YouTube channel, The LaBrant Fam. The video is called "The Live Birth Of Our Daughter" and it quickly surpassed 1 million views. 

Before Fame

She was born at 2:45 AM, weighing eight pounds and seven ounces. 

Trivia

Her name was revealed to the LaBrant family's fans through a YouTube video three days after her birth. In June of 2022, her parents made a popular video introducing her to her siblings for the first time. 

Family Life

She has three older siblings named Posie, Zealand and Everleigh. 

Associated With

In one of the photos on her posie.sunday Instagram page, she dressed up as Tigger from Winnie-the-Pooh.
