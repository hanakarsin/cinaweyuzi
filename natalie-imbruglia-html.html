Natalie Imbruglia - Age, Family, Bio

Australian singer-songwriter and actress who gained international success with her massively popular hit song "Torn." Her single "Glorious" was a #23 charting hit.

Before Fame

She acted in various commercials for known brands growing up. Her first performance was on the soap opera Neighbours as Beth Brennan in the early 1990s.

Trivia

She has earned several accolades for her singing career, including eight ARIA Awards and two Brit Awards.

Family Life

She began dating photographer Matt Field in 2017. She was married to Daniel Johns from 2003 until 2008. Prior to her marriage she dated actor David Schwimmer.

Associated With

She starred in the film Johnny English with Rowan Atkinson.
