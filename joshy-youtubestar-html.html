Joshy - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube gamer and live streamer whose Joshy channel has focused on games such as Fortnite and Roblox, and later on Omegle pranks. He's amassed over 1.3 million subscribers since his channel's inception.

Trivia

One of his most popular videos, titled "I PRETENDED TO BE 6IX9INE ON OMEGLE," has been viewed over 4.1 million times. His zodiac sign is Aries.   

Family Life

He has one older brother. 

Associated With
