Daniela Marder - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and singer who was cast as Catherine, the daughter of Katie Holmes' character Hazel, in the TV movie No Apologies. The music videos for her songs "Top Spot" and "She Gets Away With Everything," which are featured on the Funniflix YouTube channel, which has amassed over 5.5 billion views.

Before Fame

She appeared in a commercial for Lee Jeans in 2014. 

Trivia

In 2017, she was featured in the music video for the MattyB song "On My Own," which has earned over 70 million views.  A YouTube music video she was featured in on Funniflix surpassed 500 million views in 2021. The "She Gets Away With Everything" video has amassed over 786 million views. 

Family Life

She has a younger brother. 

Associated With

Other performers who have been featured on the Funniflix YouTube channel include Ivey Meeks and the Haschak Sisters. She appears in many videos with Mimi Drabik.
