Alejandra Barros - Age, Family, Bio

Mexican actress best known for playing the title character in Televisa's telenovela Mariana de la Noche.

Before Fame

She studied acting at The Reads Strasberg Theater Institute and the Actor's Studio Film & TV School, both in New York.

Trivia

She made her first appearance in a telenovela in Confidente de Secundaria in 1996.

Family Life

She had a son named Luis Miguel with her ex-husband Luis Miguel Peralta.

Associated With

She appears alongside actor Jorge Salinas in Mariana de la Noche.
