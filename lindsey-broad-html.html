Lindsey Broad - Age, Family, Bio

Actress known for appearing in television series such as The Office, 'Til Death, Hello Ladies and Kevin Can Wait.

Before Fame

She attended Penn State University where she earned a theatre degree. 

Trivia

Her film credits include 21 Jump Street, Don Jon and Get Him to the Greek.

Family Life

In 2014, she married filmmaker Sean Bradley. They have two children named Teddy and Harry.

Associated With

In The Office, she played temporary worker Cathy Simms, who filled in for Pam played by Jenna Fischer.
