Juliet Simms - Age, Family, Bio

Singer-songwriter who was a runner-up on NBC's The Voice in 2012. She joined the band Automatic Loveletter as a guitarist and lead vocalist. 

Before Fame

She began writing songs when she was 14 and signed with her first record label, Umbrella Records, when she was 16. 

Trivia

She released her first single, "Wild Child," in 2012. 

Family Life

She married Black Veil Brides singer Andy Biersack in 2016.

Associated With

Cee Lo Green was her coach on The Voice.
