Lapizito - Age, Family, Bio

Comedian and singer who first rose to fame as a member of Lapizito, Gomita, and Lapizin with his two siblings. The family appeared on the Univision show Sabadazo and then became contributors to the popular YouTube channel SoyFredy.

Before Fame

In November 2010, he opened his Twitter account with the tweet "El sábado LAPIZITO en sbadazoooo."

Trivia

His YouTube channel Lapizito Oficial features his original music. He's also popular on TikTok, where he has earned more than 13 million fans for his comedic videos on his soylapizito account. In February 2019, he shared a picture on his Instagram with singer Edwin Luna. 

Family Life

His real name is Alfredo Ordaz Campos. His sister, Aracely Ordaz Campos, portrays Gomita and his younger brother portrays Lapizin.

Associated With

Fellow YouTube comedian SKabeche appeared in his April 2016 video "Dizzy Challenge."
