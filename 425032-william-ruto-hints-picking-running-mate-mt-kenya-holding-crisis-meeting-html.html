William Ruto Hints at Picking Running Mate from Mt Kenya after Holding Crisis Meeting

  • William Ruto promised his Mt Kenya allies that he will consider picking a running mate from the region
  • He assured the leaders who attended his meeting in Nyeri that a binding agreement that would see other interests taken care of would be sealed before the polls
  • Ruto has been under pressure to pick a running mate from Central Kenya in order to solidify his support in the region

Deputy President William Ruto has hinted at picking a runningmate from the Mt Kenya region to solidify his support.

Ruto to pick his running mate from Mt Kenya region

According to a report by Daily Nation, Tanga Tanga leaders from the Mt Kenya region pressurised the DP to consider the region for the running mate slot in order to be assured of the over 6 million voters.

The DP, according to reports, agreed with the leaders but asked for more time to consult so that he could come up with a list of potential candidates to pick from.

The request was endorsed and adopted by all members who noted rushing to pick a running mate without proper consultation could lead to a fallout.

They also agreed that such premature moves could jeopardize Ruto's presidential momentum given that those who feel aggrieved by the choice might bolt out, thus exposing him to his opponents at an early stage.

The DP is said to have told his Mt Kenya allies that he would name the running mate a few weeks before the 2022 general elections.

To safeguard the interests of the Mt Kenya region, the DP promised his allies that he would enter into a binding agreement with them to ensure their needs are prioritised by his government.
