Brett Ratner - Age, Family, Bio

Director whose films included X-Men: The Last Stand and the Rush Hour films. He also served as a producer on the Fox series Prison Break.

Before Fame

He studied at New York University, earning his degree in 1990.

Trivia

He produced the comedy films Horrible Bosses and Horrible Bosses 2.

Family Life

He was born to Marcia Presman and Ronald Ratner; his mother had him when she was 16. He also has a younger brother named Max.

Associated With

He directed the movie Tower Heist, which starred Casey Affleck.
