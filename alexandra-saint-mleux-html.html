Alexandra Saint Mleux - Trivia, Family, Bio

TikTok content creator and social media personality best known for her alexandrasaintmleux account. She shares fashion modeling clips as well as art videos. She has more than 210,000 followers on the platform.

Before Fame

She was an art student prior to finding fame on social media.

Trivia

In June of 2023, she made a TikTok about her day in Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat. She created a fashion modeling video set to music made famous by Tame Impala. She also shares art photography on her alexandramalenart Instagram account.

Family Life

In June of 2023, it was reported that she was dating race car driver Charles Leclerc. 

Associated With

She created a TikTok set to music by Debbie Harry and Blondie.
