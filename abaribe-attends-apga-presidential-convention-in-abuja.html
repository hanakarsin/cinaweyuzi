Abaribe Attends APGA Presidential Convention In Abuja

Former chieftain of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Senator Enyinnaya Abaribe has been spotted at the special national convention of the All Progressives Grand Alliance (APGA) on Wednesday in Abuja.

Abaribe it would be recalled recently resigned as the Senate Minority Leader after he dumped the PDP and defected to APGA.

The APGA convention holding at the national headquarters of the party in Katampe, Abuja is to elect/confirm its 2023 presidential candidate.

The event has in attendance major party chieftains including former Minister Frank Nweke Jr and many APGA loyalists from across the country.

Naija News recalls Abaribe on Saturday, secured the senatorial ticket of the All Progressive Grand Alliance (APGA) for Abia South.

Abaribe was elected unopposed APGA’s senatorial flag bearer by a large crowd of delegates at the Azikiwe Road in Aba.

The Returning Officer, Lucky Erondu confirmed Abaribe’s victory to a standing ovation from a gathering of supporters, delegates, and party members.

Senator Abaribe in his acceptance speech appreciated the delegates for their faith in him.

The lawmaker also promised not to disappoint his constituents and vowed to sustain his fight for the enthronement of democracy, equity and justice.

He further stated that he would help return Abia State to APGA for total liberation from godfatherism and despondency.
