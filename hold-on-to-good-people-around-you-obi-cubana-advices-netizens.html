Hold On To Good People Around You

Nigerian businessman and socialite, Obinnna Iyiegbu popularly known as Obi Cubana has urged Nigerians to celebrate the people and things that matter in their life on a daily basis.

He opined that each day a person spends breathing should be seen as a huge blessing.

The businessman urged individuals to cherish the good people around them as life in itself is a blessing.

He wrote, “Hold on to good people around you. Celebrate everyone and everything you place value on, daily! Each day above the ground is a blessing!”

Cubana’s advice is coming days after the death of David’s son, Ifeanyi Adeleke who he described as a lovely, sweet boy that radiated pure joy in the three years he spent on earth.

Caroline Celebrates Billionaire Ex-Husband

Nollywood actress, Caroline Danjuma has publicly showered praises on her billionaire ex-husband, Musa Danjuma.

Naija News reports that Musa Danjuma, a businessman with interests in shipping and paint industries, turned a new age today, 4th November, 2022 and his ex-wife decided to celebrate him for his support and care.

Taking to her Instagram page, the single mum of three shared a photo of Musa Danjuma with a caption that reads “On behalf of the kids and me. I want to celebrate a delectable man, father, and friend …happy birthday my dearest .. thanks for being the best dad in the world, an amazing friend, and a support to us all in the family. May your days be long and merry “

This is coming weeks after they reunited and partied together for the sake of their daughter, Elizabeth who marked her 8th birthday.
