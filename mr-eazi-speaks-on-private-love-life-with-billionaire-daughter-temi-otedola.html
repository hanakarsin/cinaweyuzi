Mr Eazi Speaks On Private Love Life With Billionaire Daughter, Temi Otedola

Popular Nigerian singer, Oluwatosin Ajibade professionally known as Mr Eazi has opened up on his love life with his fiancee, Temi Otedola.

Speaking in a recent interview, Eazi stated that he could get married to Temi and no one would be aware due to his kind of lifestyle.

The singer recounted how he popped the question to Temi and no one was aware until he announced the news of their engagement two weeks after.

For him, he believes one shouldn’t publicize everything that goes on in their life.

He said, “I was engaged for 2 weeks before the world knew that I was engaged. So is like, I could be married right now and you wouldn’t know and maybe I amn’t just wearing my ring.

It’s just how you feel like it isn’t everything that should be outside”.

They Said I Was Having An Affair With Bobrisky – Yul Edochie

Nollywood actor, Yul Edochie has said that some netizens have alleged that he is having an affair with popular crossdresser, Bobrisky.

Yul explained that after he celebrated Bob on his birthday he suffered harsh criticism from his fans sharing the ordeal via a video uploaded to his YouTube page, Edochie said that some of his admirers flocked to the comment area to accuse him of dating the male barbie.

He said that some people also called him and insisted that he pulls down the birthday post from his page.

Edochie insisted that it is unacceptable for people to be condemned because they do not fit into the box society wants them to occupy.

He said, “Earlier this year, I wished Bobrisky a happy birthday with his picture posted on my Instagram page. And then my page literally blew up and went on fire. By the time I came back, I got thousands of messages on ….. and then my DM was flooded with messages. Somebody actually sent me a DM and said that I’m sleeping with Bobrisky. Just because I wished him a happy birthday on my page.

“Someone even called me and requested that I pull down …… Some people were even telling me how God destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah. Are you God? Did God send you to destroy Sodom and Gomorrah?”

“Stop judging people, you are not God. Bobrisky is a human being like every other person. Why do we discriminate against people? We should stop all these. God said to love your neighbour as yourself. Nigerians rest.”
