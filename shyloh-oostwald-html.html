Shyloh Oostwald - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who is widely known for her regular roles on the Nickelodeon TV series 100 Things to Do Before High School as Patti Macabre. In 2014, she debuted as the star of the TV show Spooked as Piper.

Before Fame

She was discovered by a talent scout at age 6 in an acting class.

Trivia

She made her on-screen debut during an episode of iCarly in 2010. She has provided various voices for the popular animated TV series Adventure Time.

Family Life

She was raised by her mother Lisa, who works as a talent scout in Los Angeles.

Associated With

She appeared alongside Justin Timberlake in the 2011 film In Time as Maya.
