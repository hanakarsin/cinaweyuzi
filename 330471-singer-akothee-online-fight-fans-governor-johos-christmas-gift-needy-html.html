Singer Akothee in online fight with fans over Governor Joho's Christmas gift to needy

Kenyan songstress Esther Akothee is one of the most loved celebrities in Kenya.

While the lass has millions of fans online who always support her, she also carries along with her a multitude of haters who will see fault in every move she makes.

The mother of five on Tuesday, December 2019, took to Instagram to thank Mombasa governor Hassan Joho for donating 150 bags of maize to Akothee Foundation, so they could be sent to hunger-stricken Turkana residents.

''His Excellency, May God bless you for sharing Christmas with us in Turkana Whoever is kind to the needy honors God: Proverbs 14:31. Whoever is kind to the poor lends to The Lord.'' she posted.

Some of Akothee's fans and a cross section of Kenyans were pleased with the news and thanked Joho and Akothee immensely for helping fellow Kenyans.

Others however, picked issue with the mother of five and the Mombasa governor over the donation.

Some claimed Joho did nothing by donating the 150 bags of maize as it is only a drop in his ocean of wealth.

Others stated the governor is obligated to help and Akothee should not have come out in public to heap praises on him.
