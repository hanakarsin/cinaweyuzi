Glenda Dela Cruz - Age, Family, Bio

Beauty maven and CEO of Brilliant Skin. Her first product to go viral was her now iconic charcoal soap, which she researched on her own, using her own skin problems to guide her. Her iammissglenda TikTok account is a mix of beauty, fashion, dance, and comedy. Her content has helped her accrue 3.5 million fans. 

Before Fame

She began her career selling merchandise online, such as candles and slippers. 

Trivia

Her iammissglenda Instagram account features modeling photos that focus on formal wear and evening attire. She has reels on fitness, skincare, and upcoming events. 

Family Life

She was born in the Philippines. She has spoken out about growing up in poverty and having her first child at the age of 19. She married Mart Crispher Victorio, with whom she has two sons. 

Associated With

In January of 2023, she danced to "Feels" by Calvin Harris.
