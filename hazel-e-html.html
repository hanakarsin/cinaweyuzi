May 31, 2024 Chauncey Koziol

Hazel-E - Age, Family, Bio

Cast member on the hit VH1 reality series Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood who later went on to appear on season 15 of Marriage Boot Camp alongside her husband De'Von Waller. 

Before Fame

She worked as a publicist for major talent, including Gnarls Barkley, Tank, and Soulja Boy. 

Trivia

She is also a rapper and has released a number of mixtapes and EPs. 

Family Life

She was born in Cincinnati, Ohio and graduated from Southwest Texas State University. She and De'Von Waller married in 2019 and they welcomed a daughter named Ava-Dior in June 2020. She previously dated Rose Burgundy in 2016 and she also had relationships with comedian Katt Williams and rapper Yung Berg.  

Associated With

She appeared on Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood along with Teairra Mari.
