Moha Jicho Pevu Visits Dad's Grave after Eid Prayers: &quot;It's Been 14 Years Since He Left&quot;

- Nyali legislator Mohammed Ali visited his father's grave soon after Eid prayers

- Moha's father died in 2007, and since then, he often visits the grave to pray for the departed

- The former investigative journalist admitted that he sorely misses his father

Nyali MP Mohammed Ali alias Moha Jicho Pevu has paid tribute to his late father.

The former investigative journalist visited his dad's grave soon after Eid prayers.

Photos shared by Moha depicted the legislator praying before removing the weeds around his dad’s final resting place.

Moha admitted to missing his old man while revealing he passed away 14 years ago. According to the former Radio Maisha presenter, he often visits the grave to say a prayer for the departed.

“My day with dad after Eid prayers. It’s 14yrs since he left us. 14 years of visiting his grave and making Dua for him. A time of reflection and remembrance. I miss you Dad,” he wrote.

TUKO.co.ke reported that Moha launched a radio station dubbed MO Radio.
