Debi Mazar - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who became known for her role as press agent Shauna Roberts on the hit HBO television series Entourage.

Before Fame

She briefly worked as a beautician.

Trivia

She played a small role alongside Ray Liotta in the classic mobster film Goodfellas.

Family Life

She married Gabriele Corcos in March of 2002, and the couple welcomed a daughter named Evelina in 2002 and a second daughter named Giulia in 2006.

Associated With

She appeared in music videos by Madonna for the songs "Music," "Papa Don't Preach," "True Blue," "Deeper and Deeper," and "Justify My Love."
