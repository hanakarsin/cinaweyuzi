Borussia Dortmund vs Paris Saint-Germain: Haaland's brace helps Dortmund beat PSG 2-1

- Borussia Dortmund vs Paris Saint-Germain UCL clash ended in favour of the home team

- Two second-half goals were all the hosts needed to outshine their visitors after regulation period

- Meanwhile, Neymar's lone strike was not enough to earn them a draw ahead of the second leg

German League side Borussia Dortmund have one leg into the quarterfinals of this season's Champions League championship after their victory over Paris Saint Germain.

The Bundesliga side takes a one-goal advantage into the second leg after beating the Ligue 1 side 2-1 in the first leg of their last eight stage clash at the Signal Iduna Park.

January transfer signing Erling Haaland was the hero of the match after striking twice to hand the home team the victory in front of their fans.
