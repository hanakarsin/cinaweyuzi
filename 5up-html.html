5up - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator and gamer who has gained popularity for his 5uppp Twitch account and 5up YouTube channel where he posts deception game videos, mainly playing the game Among Us. He has accumulated over 650,000 subscribers on YouTube and more than 950,000 Twitch followers.

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in August 2020. 

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos is titled "The cleanest impostor win you'll ever see..." and has received over 2 million views. His "Among Us - Submerged Custom Map (Reveal Trailer)" video has also received over 3.7 million views since June of 2021. 

Family Life

He was born in the United States. He has an older brother.

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow gamer Disguised Toast on his YouTube channel.
