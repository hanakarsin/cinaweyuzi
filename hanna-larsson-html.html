Hanna Larsson - Age, Family, Bio

Swedish singer most famous for being the younger sister of pop star Zara Larsson. She has performed as a member of the singing duo Hanna & Andrea. 

Before Fame

She grew up in Sweden.

Trivia

Her cover of Taylor Swift's "Shake It Off" has been viewed more than 150,000 times on YouTube. Her hannaxlarsson Instagram account has become widely popular, earning a following of over 80,000 fans. She and Zara are incredibly close.

Family Life

Her mother's name is Agnetha and her father's name is Anders. Her sister Zara won the talent show Talang in 2008.

Associated With

She covered Tove Lo's "Paradise" for Hanna & Andrea's YouTube channel.
