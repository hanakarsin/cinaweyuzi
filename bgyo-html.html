BGYO - Members, Ages, Trivia

Five-member Filipino boy group who formed through ABS-CBN's talent show program Star Hunt Academy. Their debut single "The Light" released in early 2021 alongside an accompanying music video.

Trivia

They have been featured on the Billboard Next Big Sound chart. They partnered with the streaming platform Kumu in 2021. 

Members

The group is composed of Gelo, Akira, JL, Mikki, and Nate.
