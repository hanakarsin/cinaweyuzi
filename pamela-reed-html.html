Enda Jantzen

Pamela Reed - Age, Family, Bio

Star of the 1990 Arnold Schwarzenegger comedy Kindergarten Cop who also played Marlene Knope in Parks and Recreation.

Before Fame

She received a Bachelors in Fine Arts at the University of Washington.

Trivia

Her children Reed and Lily were both adopted.

Family Life

She married Sand Smolan in 1988 and the couple adopted two children.

Associated With

She voiced Ruth Powers, the Simpson family's next-door neighbor in several episodes of Matt Groening's "The Simpsons."
