Elizabeth Kim (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber known for her appearances on the Mightyduck World vlogging channel. She has gained popularity there for appearing in family-themed vlogs alongside her husband Desmond English.

Before Fame

She co-created the Mightyduck World vlogging channel on February 14, 2014.

Trivia

She has helped her YouTube channel amass more than 2.5 million subscribers since its beginning.

Family Life

Social media star Skunky is her younger brother. She and her husband documented her pregnancy and birth of their son, Channing, on their YouTube channel. In September 2018, they welcomed a daughter named Emmalyn.

Associated With

She is the sister-in-law, and frequent online collaborator, of Mighty Neicy.
