Why are you resorting to witchcraft? Embu leaders to Raila Odinga

A section of Jubilee leaders have now claimed CORD leader Raila Odinga is using demonic powers to oust President Uhuru Kenyatta in 2017.

READ ALSO: Ferdinand Waititu's son almost kills Kisumu Senator Anyang Nyong'o

The leaders while speaking in Embu said the recent visit by Raila to a traditional shrine in Nyanza was meant to earn him some supernatural powers to be president next year, Citizen Digital reports.

The Jubilee leaders said Raila recently visited the Got Ramogi shrine in Siaya County where a traditional ritual was performed to strengthen him politically and to be able to remove Uhuru from power.

Embu Senator Lenny Kivuti told Raila that such practices were outdated and satanic noting that many leaders were seeking powers from known sorcerers ahead of 2017.

The leaders were speaking at the sidelines of a fund raising at Karuri Catholic Church in Manyatta.

On her part, Embu Women Rep Rose Mitaru also lambasted politicians who seek demonic powers to raise into power saying such leaders were setting bad examples to the young people looking up to them.

Another Jubilee Party presidential campaign member Kalembe Ndile also claimed that the fact that Raila emerged from the shrine wearing a skin cloth, a traditional headgear and holding a two headed spear were sure signs that he has undergone a sinister ritual.
