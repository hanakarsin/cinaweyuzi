Jackie Aina - Age, Family, Bio

Makeup and beauty guru previously known as MakeupGameOnPoint and LilPumpkinPie05 who became a licensed cosmetologist after graduating from Vidal Sassoon Academy. She also started her own lifestyle brand called FORVR. 

Before Fame

She began pursuing makeup art as a profession in her young adult years, beginning to take makeup seriously in 2009. She worked for MAC and Bobbi Brown cosmetics and freelanced as a makeup artist for five years.

Trivia

She is a US Army veteran. Her YouTube channel has more than 3.5 million subscribers.  She was featured in YouTube's livestream shopping event in November of 2021. She has also created a lifestyle blog at jackaina.com. 

Family Life

She is a Los Angeles-based makeup artist of Nigerian descent. She has sisters named Toyin and Folake. She started dating Densal; he's known on Instagram as denisasamoah. She is also known as Naira Banks.

Associated With

In August of 2012, she posted an "Aaliyah More Than a Woman Makeup Tutorial."
