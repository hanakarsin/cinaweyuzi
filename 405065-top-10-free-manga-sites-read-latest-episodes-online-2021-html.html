Top 10 free Manga sites to read latest episodes online in 2021

If you are a Manga fan, you might have probably encountered the frustrations and difficulty in finding your favourite comics online unless you know the best free Manga sites. Such sites can be hard to find, especially since most are not legal and keep changing their servers. Even if you are lucky to find a few legal Manga sites, it may end up being a waste of your time if they do not have the latest episodes for you to read.

Manga originated from Japan, where they publish a comic series that showcases their ancient culture. The problem arises with the many hurdles when trying to access these comic stories online.

1. BookWalker

BookWalker is held in high regard by Manga fans in Japan and beyond. To most, this is the best free Manga reader both for beginners and life-long fans of the comics. The site’s domain belongs to Kadokawa as the company's official digital bookstore and software created for readers of Light Novels and digital Manga series.

Simply put, BookWalker is an eBook store designed with Manga and light novel readers in mind. Having established that this is a genuine and legal platform, you will want to know what it will cost you to use the services. The site is absolutely free without any subscription fee.

2. Comixology

If you have been reading traditional comics such as Marvel or DC, you must have come across Comixology. This site is owned by Amazon and used by most North American Manga publishers to present their digital titles.

VIZ Manga has quite a stake in Manga being among the top publishers of English-language Manga. Some of the most notable works they have published include Naruto, One Piece and One-Punch Man. While most other Manga reading sites require you to create an account and sign-in every time you access any content, MangaReader is completely the opposite. Its interface is basic without any unnecessary stuff like logging in requirements to read your favourite comic.

9. Crunchyroll

Although not a strictly Manga dedicated website, Crunchyroll is still among the most preferred by comic readers. It is a streaming website, but it projects Manga separately to make it easier for users to navigate. One of the site's strengths is its fast and straightforward user-interface that is user-friendly even for small children. It also has a unique forum page that boasts a separate discussion division for Manga. Users can even create a poll to spice up their experience. The site has a bookmark feature through which you can queue your favourite comics to read later.

10. Zingbox

Manga reading has rapidly shifted from desktops and personal computers to smartphones. This is thanks to websites like Zingbox that have created applications for IOS and Android devices.
