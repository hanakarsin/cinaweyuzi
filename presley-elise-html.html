Presley Elise - Age, Family, Bio

Social star, model and cheerleader who has earned over 100,000 followers to her presleyelise Instagram account. She was named LA Perfect Cheerleader 2016-2017 and Little Miss Louisiana United States 2017-2018. She has often posted short dance videos, POV style lip synchs and outfit photos to her account. 

Before Fame

She was two years old when she started to dance and four years old when she started to cheer. 

Trivia

She became a Cheerbrats Collection representative as well as a Rebel Athletic Junior Model. She has appeared in numerous ads for local businesses as well as for global designers such as Elisa B. She has also posted a paid advertisement of herself wearing leggings from the J80 Fit athletic line. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. She has a younger brother. 

Associated With

She took a photo with Sophia Pashow as seen on her own Instagram. She was cast as the lead in a music video for Mason Coutinho in 2018. She and Amira Harvey have been seen on each other's social media accounts.
