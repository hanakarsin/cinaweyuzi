Reactions As Video Of Traffic Officers Who Arrested A Soldier Surfaces On Social Media

A video of some road traffic officers been dealt with after arresting a soldier driving against  traffic has surfaced on social media.

Naija News understands that the traffic officers who tried to enforce the law by ensuring no one is above it, got more than what they bargained for.

In the now viral video, some of the traffic officers who were pictured arresting a yet-to-be identified military man were all seen been punished.
