Davido's Baby Mama Chioma Wows Many As She Ditches Braids And Long Hair Lifestyle To Join Low-Cut Ga

Chioma Avril Rowland, the third baby mama of Nigerian singer Davido, is about to take over the town with her new stunning look.

Chioma has ditched the braids and long hair lifestyle to join the low-cut gang and she looks absolutely beautiful.

The low-cut and tinted colour hairstyle is a recent trend making waves among many Nigerians and celebrities.

The mum of one who has always had her hair out in different hairstyles decided to go a fashionable low-cut and she looks just as stunning as ever.

Chef Chi as she’s fondly called took to her Instagram page to share a video of herself proudly rocking the new hairstyle.

Davido’s baby mama is also seen rocking a purple dress as she gushes over her beauty.

Despite dumping the braid and long hairstyles for a long time, Chioma still look beautiful with the low-cut.
