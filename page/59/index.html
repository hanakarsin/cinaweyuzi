Buhari To Sign 2022 Budget As NASS Sends Appropriation Bill

Apr 17, 2024 President Muhammadu Buhari on Tuesday received the 2022 Appropriation Bill from the National Assembly. It also rained the oil benchmark from the proposed $57 per barrel to $62. According to The PUNCH, a communication from the Clerk to the National Assembly, Ojo Amos, disclos that NASS sent the budget to the presidency on Friday and it was received by the Office of the Chief of Staff to the President on Saturday.
