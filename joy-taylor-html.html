Joy Taylor - Age, Family, Bio

Sports reporter and blogger most well known as an on-air host for FS1 and Fox Sports Radio. She also blogs about sports, entertainment, and other lifestyle topics on her website JoyTaylorTalks.com.

Before Fame

She got her start hosting the radio show The Noise at her alma mater, Barry University, eventually serving as the station manager for WBRY Radio while completing her bachelor's in broadcasting.

Trivia

She previously hosted Thursday Night Live and Fantasy Football Today on CBSSports.com.

Family Life

Her older brother, Jason Taylor, rose to fame as a linebacker for the Miami Dolphins.

Associated With

She worked with a number of radio professionals, including DJ Laz, before earning her spot hosting The Ticket on 104.3FM.
