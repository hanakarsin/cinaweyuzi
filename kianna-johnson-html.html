Kianna Jay - Age, Family, Bio

Became known as one half of the YouTube channel Keion and Kianna alongside her now ex-boyfriend Keion. The channel has become known for featuring challenges, stories, pranks, vlogs, and countless other videos. She is also popular on Instagram where she is known by the handle itspapi____. She has also posted many dance videos to Hip-Hop songs on TikTok. 

Before Fame

Before joining YouTube in 2017, she began posting photos to Instagram in May 2015. 

Trivia

She later changed her joint channel name to Its KiannaJay and featured prank and challenge-related content. She dressed up as Buzz Lightyear for Halloween in 2021 and posted a photo of it on Instagram. 

Family Life

She and Keion began dating in 2014; but broke up in 2018. She dated Ynh Primetime from 2019 to 2020. She hails from the San Francisco bay area.

Associated With

She has grown popular on a couples YouTube prank and vlog channel like JoelTV.
