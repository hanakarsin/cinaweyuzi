Former Housemate 'Shamelessly' Begs Big Brother To Join Show

Former ‘Level Up’ housemate, Hermes has put shame aside to publicly beg Big Brother to invite him to join the on-going All Stars edition of the show.

Naija News reports that selected housemates from previous editions of the show, like Mercy, WhiteMoney, Venita, Neo, Ike, Cee-C, Cross, Angel and Alex, Seyi, Kiddwaya, among others, returned to the reality TV show on Sunday, 23rd, July 2023.

However, Hermes was amongst those who were not invited back to the show.

Pleading for a chance to be back at the show, Hermes via his Twitter and Instagram page on Sunday begged publicly to be invited back to the show.

The reality star said, he wants to show the world how much the show ‘changed his life.’

He concluded that he needed to be in the show and participating with other housemates.

Sharing the video, he captioned it; “@BBNaija. call me bro…@coolesttribe. the campaign is still on #gethermesinthehouse, nah unah hand I Dey o. @multichoice_group no wan pick my call.”
