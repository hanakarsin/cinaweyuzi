BBNaija's Nengi Gets People Talking Over What She Did To Vee

Former Big Brother Naija ‘LockDown’ Housemate Nengi Rebbecca Hampson is currently trending on the microblogging platform Twitter over what she did to her colleague Vee Iye.

Nengi took to her Snapchat to name her ‘loves’ using a picture of herself, Laycon, Vee, Dorathy and Neo.

However, Nengi got netizens talking for cropping out Vee and Dorathy from the group photo.

Recall Vee and Nengi were once good friends, but many believed that the duo’s friendship was because they share a common enemy, Erica.

On the contrary, some netizens are speculating that the friendship of Nengi and Vee is one-sided, considering how they show support for each other.

Some Twitter threads claimed that Nengi does not fake her friendship like Vee and exerts the same energy she gets from other people.
