'I Created Notorious Cult Group Terrorising Lagos, Ogun Residents'

Fast-rising street sensation, Habeeb Okikiola, popularly known as Portable has claimed that he founded ‘One Million Boys’, a cult group terrorizing Lagos and Ogun states residents.

The singer in a video shared online also claimed that he formed the Ajah Boys, adding that people can confirm from someone, Sammy Larry.

Portable in the video said, “Open your ears and hear me, Have you heard about Ajah Boys, One Million Boys, I’m their founder. Go and ask Sammy larry.”

The controversial singer spoke in Yoruba in the video.

This is coming months after Portable was spotted in a video directing some boys to beat his former disc jockey, DJ Chicken.

Portable who spoke in Yoruba in the viral video ordered guys to beat up the DJ for allegedly sending a message to his wife.

The development saw the Ogun State police command order the singer to report himself or get arrested.

Portable, however, reported to the state police headquarters, Eleweran, Abeokuta a week after the invite.

The spokesman of the command, Abimbola Oyeyemi said portable was interrogated and later granted bail.
