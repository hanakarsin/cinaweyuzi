Jayingee - Age, Family, Bio

Roblox gamer notable for his trolling, pranking and overall funny videos featuring gameplay from the popular massively multiplayer online role playing game. He has earned over 1.5 million subscribers. His "I RUINED this ROBLOX HOSPITAL'S TRAINING with ADMIN POWERS!" video has received over 8 million views since it was published in April of 2019.

His first video, published in March of 2017, was titled "Pokémon Brick Bronze - Gym Leader Ryan (Ground Type)."

Trivia

Family Life

Associated With
