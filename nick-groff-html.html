Nick Groff - Age, Family, Bio

Co-founder of the Ghost Adventures Crew who is best recognized for starring on the Travel Channel's Ghost Adventures. He also produced the series Vegas Stripped and directed the 2004 film Malevolence.

Before Fame

He met future ghost hunter Aaron Goodwin while studying film at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.

Trivia

Ghost Adventures was originally an independent film that focused on paranormal activity in Virginia City, Nevada.

Family Life

He shared a house in New England with his wife, Veronique Groff.

Associated With

He has co-starred on Ghost Adventures with Zak Bagans since the show first aired in 2008.
