SMG4 - Age, Family, Bio

Named Luke Lerdwichagul, he is a Super Mario 64 machinimist and creator of Super Mario 64 bloopers who runs the channel supermarioglitchy4, or SMG4 for short. The channel has earned over 5 million subscribers. He has a secondary account called Hobo Bros featuring hacks, memes and more. 

Before Fame

His first blooper video was titled "super mario 64 blooper short: The cake is a lie!" He uploaded it on May 7, 2011. 

Trivia

His "super mario 64 bloopers: Who let the chomp out?" video accumulated more than 10 million views in just 7 months. He has also made a number of videos where he tells stories and explains his thinking behind his videos. 

Family Life

He is from Australia and has a brother named Kevin, as well as a sister named Tish.

Associated With

He posts Super Mario content like Luke Suliman, Logan Thirtyacre, and SullyPwnz.
