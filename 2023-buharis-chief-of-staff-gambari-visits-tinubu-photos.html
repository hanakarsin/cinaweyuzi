Buharis Chief Of Staff, Gambari Visits Tinubu (Photos)

Professor Ibrahim Gambari, the Chief of Staff to President Muhammadu Buhari, has visited the National Leader of All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

Naija News reports that Gambari visited Tinubu at his Asokoro residence in Abuja on Monday, few hours the APC leader visited President Buhari in Aso Rock.

Speaking to State House correspondents on Sunday, Tinubu said he was at the Presidential Villa to thank the President for visiting him in London some months ago where he sought medical treatment.

Recall that Tinubu had met with the lawmaker representing Sokoto North Senatorial District, Aliyu Wamakko, at his residence on Sunday.

It was gathered that the meeting between Tinubu and the former Governor of Sokoto State was held at Wamakko’s residence in Asokoro, Abuja.

Wamakko while speaking during the visit expressed his appreciation to the former Governor of Lagos State for his kind gesture.

The lawmaker described Tinubu as a man of integrity for promoting peace and harmony among various political parties across Nigeria.

He also promised to support the presidential ambition of the APC leader ahead of the 2023 general elections.

Wamakko stated that Tinubu has the quality to rule Nigeria and make it a better nation for both the poor and less privileged.
