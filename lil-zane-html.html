Lil Zane - Age, Family, Bio

Born Zane Copeland, he is an Atlanta-based rapper best remembered for the 2000 hit single "Callin Me." His album titled "Young World: The Future" was No. 165 on the Billboard 200 he sold more than 5,000 copies.

Before Fame

He started rapping with his cousins and local kids at age ten, he was inspired by Another Bad Creation and Kris Kross.

Trivia

He had small roles in the films The Fighting Temptations (2003), Dr. Dolittle 2 (2001), Cuttin' da Mustard, and The Parkers.

Family Life

He married Demiqua Sa'Sha Brumfield in 2001.

Associated With

He collaborated on the song "Hardball" with Lil Wayne.
