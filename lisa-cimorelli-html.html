Lisa Cimorelli - Age, Family, Bio

Multi-instrumentalist who plays keyboard, drums and guitar for the band Cimorelli. She also helps arrange songs for the band. 

Before Fame

She was a founding member of Cimorelli in 2007, which was discovered by a mainstream audience via YouTube. 

Trivia

She has over 390,000 followers on Twitter and is also active on Tumblr and Instagram. 

Family Life

She has three older siblings and seven younger siblings. In 2019, she got engaged to Chad Gilbert and the two wed on October 3, 2020. Their daughter Lily was born on July 17, 2021.

Associated With

She formed Cimorelli alongside her sisters Christina, Katherine, Dani, Lauren and Amy.
