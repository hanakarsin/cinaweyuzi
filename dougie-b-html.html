Dougie B - Age, Family, Bio

Bronx drill rapper known for his collaborations with other Bronx-based rappers such as B-Lovee and Kay Flock. They have collaborated on songs such as "Brotherly Love" and "Pop That." 

Before Fame

Though he grew up in The Bronx, he attended elementary school in Yonkers. 

Trivia

He has over 290,000 followers on his dougieb___ Instagram account, where he has posted clips from his music as well as pictures from his day-to-day endeavors, including parties he has attended. 

Family Life

His real name is Douglas Johnson. When he was 15 years old, his father passed away. 

Associated With

Inspirations of his have included Chief Keef and Kodak Black.
