APC State Chapter Gives Condition To Support Tinubu

Members of the All Progressives Congress (APC) in Zamfara State have disclosed that candidates of the party across all levels have a role to play in order to get their votes in the 2023 general elections.

They hinted that while their loyalty to the party is unwavering, there are expectations they also have from the candidates.

Specifically, the party members said the APC presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu must commit to tackling the insecurity in the state before they can pledge their support.

Naija News reports their position was made known in a statement issued on Thursday by the APC Zonal Publicity Secretary, North West, Mallam Musa Mada.

“As the fact-finding tour of the North West Zonal officials of All Progressives Congress reached Zamfara on Wednesday, the stakeholders of the party in the State assured the Zonal Chairman, Mallam Salihu Lukman and his team that they have unflinching loyalty to the party in whatever course and they are united in all fronts to give the party the required result necessary to claim the state in totality.

“Yet, they demand the commitment of the presidential and all other candidates vying for electoral posts from the party for them to deliver the state to them.

“The party’s stakeholders demand a serious commitment on the deteriorating security situation in the State and the region from anybody vying for any electoral post, especially the presidential candidate from the party to work for his success,” they said in the statement.
