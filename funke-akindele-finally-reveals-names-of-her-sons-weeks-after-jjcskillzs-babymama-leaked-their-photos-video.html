Funke Akindele Finally Reveals Names Of Her Sons Weeks After JJCSkillz's Babymama Leaked Their Photo

Award-winning Nigerian actress Funke Akindele has finally opened up on the names of her twin sons weeks after JJC Skillz’s baby mama, Mella leaked their pictures online.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Mella had exposed the faces of Funke Akindele twins as a warning for her not to reveal her family’s secret.

The issue between the duo came to light when Mella’s son Benito alleged that Funke Akindele was toxic when he lived with her.

According to him: “She’s not what you all think she is, I lived with her for 2 years, It was horror.”

The 17-year-old claim had led to a series of events, one of them being the exposure of the faces of the twins.

However, it seems Funke has accepted the exposure of the children’s identity as she has proceeded to reveal their names, Zach and Zion during the AMVCA awards.
