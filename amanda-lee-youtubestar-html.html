AmandaRachLee - Age, Family, Bio

Canadian YouTuber who has become known for her art channel AmandaRachLee where she teaches her viewers about drawing, calligraphy and journaling. She has also been a creator for Much.com. She also created her own stationary line called Shop AmandaRachLee. 

Before Fame

She created her YouTube channel in January 2013. 

Trivia

Her YouTube channel currently has more than 2.2 million subscribers. She had brand deal with the Samsung and posted a promotional watch party video for the company on her YouTube channel. 

Family Life

She has a brother and a sister. 

Associated With

She collaborated on a video with Adelaine Morin and she interviewed actors Jon Hamm, Ed Helms and Jeremy Renner.
