Jaden Cradle - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator on TikTok notable for his relatable vlogs, lip-sync and comedy videos. He accrued more than 1.5 million followers on his former jaden_cradle account. He has also shared multiple dance videos with his friends in his school hallway on his Instagram page. 

Before Fame

He debuted on TikTok in the summer of 2019 with a video called "What makes dudes go crazy #firstvideo #fyp #foru #trend my bad if my edits were off."

Trivia

He had an Instagram account with a handle of foeJaden, but changed his username to self-titled. He was known on Snapchat as jcradle8. His self-titled YouTube channel has featured multiple videos of him going on the website Omegle at 3 in the morning. He has also posted outfit photos and photos of himself shirtless on his Instagram. 

Family Life

He posted a TikTok video with his mother on June 27, 2020. 

Associated With
