Photos Of Actor Terry Crews Illustrating Magazine Cover Take Internet By Storm

The Internet can't get enough of these photos of Terry Crew illustrating a magazine cover.

Terry Crews is more talented than you. 

I mean, not only can the man do this:

And not only is he responsible for the funniest thing to ever happen in a Wayans brother anything:

But, in addition to being an incredible actor, is also a heck of an illustrator. Which was on full display for his cover of the latest Ad Age magazine he drew himself.

Yes that's right, Crews is a heck of an artist. Don't believe me? Here he is posing with his handiwork. He's so proud of his work, and who can blame him?! He absolutely nailed it, and we love how much he loves not only the art, but also the sense of accomplishment that comes with a job well done. We are pretty inspired by you, TC. 

In fact, Terry Crews is such a multifaceted man of several talents that people are actually saying that the whole thing is unfair. And they're right, you know? How is it that a single person is so good at literally everything he attempts? He's kind, he's smart, he's talented (in so many different ways!!!), he's handsome. The list just goes on and on. 

Crews has been vocal before about his degree in fine arts and went to college on both artistic and athletic scholarships. Actually one of his first jobs was as a sketch artist for local TV news stations.

Oh and in case you didn't know, the man also managed to design some pretty incredible looking furniture as well.

I know, I know, he really does it all.

The stuff's actually really good. I'd totally put this in my house.

Oh and you can add being a generally amazing guy to his long list of talents, too, as people's personal anecdotes of him will tell you.

But his characters contained references to the man's talents all along.
